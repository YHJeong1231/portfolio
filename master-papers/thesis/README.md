- **Title:** Backpropagation- and LMI-based Neuro-Adaptive Observer for a Class of Uncertain Nonlinear Sytems
- **Institution:** GIST, Department of Mechanical Engineering
- **Year:** 2025
- **Advisor:** Prof. Pilwon Heo
- **PDF:** [Download](Master_Thesis.pdf)
- **Abstract:** This thesis presents an LMI-based neuro-adaptive observer with backpropagation learning for uncertain nonlinear discrete-time systems. The method directly designs the observer in discrete time, avoiding instability from discretization, and uses a single-hidden-layer neural network to approximate unknown dynamics. Stability is guaranteed through Lyapunov analysis and convex LMI optimization, while the number of tuning parameters is reduced by adopting a common observer gain structure. An $H_\infty$ formulation further suppresses approximation errors and measurement noise. Simulations on a PMSM compressor model confirm accurate state and disturbance estimation and demonstrate robustness compared to conventional approaches.
